Animals in Antarctica

Antarctica is home to multiple species of birds, many marine animals, and some invertebrates such as flies, beetles, mites, and nematodes. There are no terrestrial mammals, reptiles, or amphibians.

Here is a list of some of the animals that you can find in Antarctica:

  • Adelie Penguin
     
  • Chinstrap Penguin
     
  • Gentoo Penguin
     
  • Emperor Penguin
     
  • Wandering Albatross
     
  • Antarctic Skua
     
  • Snow Petrel
     
  • Yellow-billed Pintail
     
  • Fur Seal
     
  • Leopard Seal
     
  • Elephant Seal
     
  • Weddell Seal
     
  • Humpback Whale
     
  • Minke Whale
     
  • Blue Whale
     
  • Killer Whale
     
  • Krill
     
  • Antarctic Toothfish
     
  • Icefish
     
  • Antarctic Midge
